Design guardrails:
- White rounded cards on warm off-white background.
- Faux soft shadows are editable offset shapes, not raster effects.
- Acid lime, orange, blue, and mint are used as small semantic accents.
- No decorative blobs, gradients, photos, screenshots, or placeholder copy.
